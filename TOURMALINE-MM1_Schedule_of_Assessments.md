# TOURMALINE-MM1 Schedule of Assessments
## NCT01564537

### Trial Overview
- **Population:** Relapsed and/or Refractory Multiple Myeloma (RRMM)
- **Phase:** Phase 3, Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled
- **Sample Size:** N ≈ 722 (360 ixazomib-Rd, 362 placebo-Rd)

### Treatment Regimen (28-day cycles)
- **Ixazomib (or placebo):** 4.0 mg orally on Days 1, 8, 15
- **Lenalidomide:** 25 mg orally on Days 1-21 (or 10 mg if CrCl ≤60 mL/min)
- **Dexamethasone:** 40 mg orally on Days 1, 8, 15, 22

## Special Assessment Notes

### Pharmacokinetic (PK) Sampling
- **Population:** Subset of patients (sparse sampling)
- **Timing:** Selected cycles (typically Cycle 1)
- **Samples:** Pre-dose and post-dose time points
- **Purpose:** Population PK modeling

### Biomarker Assessments

#### Blood Samples (Baseline only)
- Germline DNA for genetic variants (PSMB1, TRAF-3, NFKB pathway genes)
- Circulating proteasome levels
- RNA analysis (optional)
- Plasma/serum biomarkers

#### Tumor Samples (Bone Marrow - Baseline)
- Gene expression profiling
- Somatic mutations (KRAS, NRAS, BRAF, TP53, etc.)
- High-risk cytogenetics confirmation
- Relapse samples if applicable

### Response Assessment Criteria (IMWG)

Assessed every 4 weeks by Independent Review Committee (IRC):

- **Stringent CR (sCR):** CR + normal FLC ratio + absent clonal plasma cells
- **Complete Response (CR):** Negative immunofixation, <5% bone marrow plasma cells
- **Very Good Partial Response (VGPR):** ≥90% M-protein reduction
- **Partial Response (PR):** ≥50% M-protein reduction
- **Minimal Response (MR):** 25-49% M-protein reduction
- **Stable Disease (SD):** Not meeting criteria for other categories
- **Progressive Disease (PD):** ≥25% increase in M-protein or new lesions

### Dose Modifications

Dose holds and reductions based on:
- **Hematologic toxicity:** Thrombocytopenia, neutropenia, anemia
- **Non-hematologic toxicity:** Rash, neuropathy, GI toxicity, fatigue
- **Renal function changes**

## Key Timepoints for AI/ML Modeling

### Baseline (Pre-Treatment)
- Demographics, disease characteristics, ISS staging
- Cytogenetics, genomics, biomarkers
- Baseline organ function, performance status
- Baseline M-protein, free light chains

### Treatment Phase (Longitudinal)
- **Dosing Data:** Actual doses given (Days 1, 8, 15 each cycle)
- **Dose Modifications:** Holds, reductions, discontinuations with dates and reasons
- **PK Data:** Drug concentrations if available
- **PD Biomarkers:** M-protein, free light chains (every 4 weeks)
- **Hematologic Markers:** CBC (Days 1, 8, 15 for Cycles 1-3, then Day 1)
- **Safety Labs:** Chemistry panels
- **Adverse Events:** Grade, onset, duration, relationship to study drug
- **Response Assessments:** Every 4 weeks per IMWG

### Outcomes
- **Progression-Free Survival (PFS):** Time to progression or death
- **Overall Survival (OS):** Time to death
- **Adverse Events:** All treatment-emergent AEs with grading
- **Quality of Life:** EORTC scores over time

## Data Request Considerations

When requesting data, ensure you obtain:

1. **Complete dosing records** with exact dates and doses for all three drugs
2. **All longitudinal laboratory data** at protocol-specified timepoints
3. **Adverse event data** with onset times, grades, and relationship to dosing
4. **Response assessments** at each timepoint
5. **Genomic/cytogenetic data** if available
6. **PK data** from PK substudies (if conducted)
7. **Time-to-event data** with censoring information
8. **Data dictionaries** and variable definitions
9. **Protocol amendments** that may have changed assessment schedules
